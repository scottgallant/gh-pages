Jennifer Tasker

Jennifer Tasker has worked as a Research Assistant for the CHBMR since 2014.

She graduated from UPEI with a Bachelor Honours degree in English Literature in 2009 and received a Masters degree in English Literature from McMaster University in 2011.

She provides research and writing support for all of the CHBMR projects.
