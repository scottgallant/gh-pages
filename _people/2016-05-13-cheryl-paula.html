Cheryl Paula

Cheryl has been the Administrative Assistant for the CHBMR since 2014. She is responsible for administrative and clerical tasks and is the first point of contact for the Centre.

She brings over thirty years of administrative experience to the CHBMR and has worked in academic, government, and industry settings.
